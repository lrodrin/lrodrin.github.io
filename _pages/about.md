She holds a Master’s degree in Artificial Intelligence from Universidad Internacional Menéndez Pelayo ([UIMP](https://www.uimp.es/en/), 2022) and a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya ([UPC](https://www.upc.edu/en?set_language=en), 2017). She is a Data Engineer in the Data Office Department at Universitat Oberta de Catalunya ([UOC](https://www.uoc.edu/en)), where her work focuses on data engineering, analytics, and building interoperable tools and models for the [DS4Skills](https://www.skillsdataspace.eu/) project, contributing to the creation of a trusted European Data Space for Skills. Her main research interests include data engineering, applied artificial intelligence, data analytics, and the development of interoperable tools and infrastructures for skills and education data.
